ACME Corporation Employee Handbook - 2024 Edition

CHAPTER 1: COMPANY OVERVIEW

ACME Corporation was founded in 1985 by John Smith and Mary Johnson in Silicon Valley. The company
specializes in manufacturing innovative gadgets and industrial equipment. Our headquarters is located
at 1234 Innovation Drive, San Jose, California 95134.

Our Mission: To provide cutting-edge solutions that improve everyday life while maintaining the
highest standards of quality and customer satisfaction.

Our Values:
- Innovation: We constantly push the boundaries of what's possible
- Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency
- Excellence: We strive for the highest quality in everything we do
- Teamwork: We believe in collaboration and mutual respect

CHAPTER 2: EMPLOYMENT POLICIES

2.1 Working Hours
Standard working hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. The company offers flexible
working arrangements including:
- Flexible start times between 7:00 AM and 10:00 AM
- Remote work options (up to 3 days per week with manager approval)
- Compressed work weeks (4x10 schedule available for eligible positions)

2.2 Paid Time Off (PTO)
- New employees receive 15 days of PTO per year
- After 3 years: 20 days per year
- After 5 years: 25 days per year
- After 10 years: 30 days per year

PTO can be used for vacation, personal days, or sick leave. Unused PTO up to 5 days can be carried
over to the next year.

2.3 Holidays
ACME Corporation observes the following paid holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day and the day after
- Christmas Eve and Christmas Day

2.4 Benefits
Full-time employees are eligible for:
- Health insurance (medical, dental, vision) - company pays 80% of premiums
- 401(k) retirement plan with 4% company match
- Life insurance (2x annual salary)
- Short-term and long-term disability insurance
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Tuition reimbursement up to $5,000 per year
- Gym membership subsidy ($50/month)

CHAPTER 3: EXPENSE POLICY

3.1 Travel Expenses
- Airfare: Economy class for flights under 6 hours, business class for longer flights
- Hotels: Up to $200/night domestic, $300/night international
- Meals: Up to $75/day domestic, $100/day international
- Rental cars: Compact or intermediate class unless business need requires larger

3.2 Expense Reporting
All expenses must be submitted within 30 days of incurring them. Receipts are required for all
expenses over $25. Expense reports are submitted through the ExpenseTrack system and require
manager approval for amounts over $500.

3.3 Corporate Credit Cards
Corporate credit cards are available for employees who travel frequently (more than 4 trips per
year). Monthly statements must be reconciled within 15 days of receipt.

CHAPTER 4: IT AND SECURITY POLICIES

4.1 Password Requirements
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
- Passwords must be changed every 90 days
- Cannot reuse last 10 passwords

4.2 Data Classification
- Public: Information that can be freely shared
- Internal: For employees only, not for external distribution
- Confidential: Sensitive business information, restricted access
- Restricted: Highly sensitive data (PII, financial data), strictly controlled

4.3 Remote Access
VPN is required for all remote access to company systems. Two-factor authentication is mandatory.
Company-issued laptops must have full disk encryption enabled.

CHAPTER 5: PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT

5.1 Performance Reviews
- Annual performance reviews conducted in Q1 each year
- Mid-year check-ins in July
- Continuous feedback encouraged through our Feedback360 platform

5.2 Rating Scale
- Exceeds Expectations: Consistently delivers exceptional results
- Meets Expectations: Reliably achieves goals and objectives
- Needs Improvement: Performance gaps that require attention
- Unsatisfactory: Significant performance issues

5.3 Compensation
Annual merit increases are based on performance ratings and market adjustments. The typical range
is 2-5% for "Meets Expectations" and 5-10% for "Exceeds Expectations".
